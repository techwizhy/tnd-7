Explore classic and modern political thought, key ideologies (liberalism, socialism, conservatism, nationalism), and the philosophical foundations of human rights and justice systems.

  • Modern Ideologies and Post-Colonial thought
  • Ethics in political action and decision-making

Analyze how policies are created, evaluated, and implemented. Study economic models, administrative challenges, and the impact of public opinion on policy choices.

  • Policy Cycle: Formulation to Evaluation
